  6  """ASTRAL RAF (Rapid Access Format) Sequence Maps. 
  7   
  8  The ASTRAL RAF Sequence Maps record the relationship between the PDB SEQRES 
  9  records (representing the sequence of the molecule used in an experiment) to 
 10  the ATOM records (representing the atoms experimentally observed). 
 11   
 12  This data is derived from the Protein Data Bank CIF files. Known errors in the 
 13  CIF files are corrected manually, with the original PDB file serving as the 
 14  final arbiter in case of discrepancies. 
 15   
 16  Residues are referenced by residue ID. This consists of a the PDB residue 
 17  sequence number (up to 4 digits) and an optional PDB insertion code (an 
 18  ascii alphabetic character, a-z, A-Z). e.g. "1", "10A", "1010b", "-1" 
 19   
 20  See "ASTRAL RAF Sequence Maps":http://astral.stanford.edu/raf.html 
 21   
 22  Dictionary `protein_letters_3to1` provides a mapping from the 3-letter amino 
 23  acid codes found in PDB files to 1-letter codes.  The 3-letter codes include
