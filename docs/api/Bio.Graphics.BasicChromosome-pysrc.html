  8  """Draw representations of organism chromosomes with added information.

 10  These classes are meant to model the drawing of pictures of chromosomes. 
 11  This can be useful for lots of things, including displaying markers on 
 12  a chromosome (ie. for genetic mapping) and showing syteny between two 
 13  chromosomes. 
 14   
 15  The structure of these classes is intended to be a Composite, so that 
 16  it will be easy to plug in and switch different parts without 
 17  breaking the general drawing capabilities of the system. The 
 18  relationship between classes is that everything derives from 
 19  _ChromosomeComponent, which specifies the overall interface. The parts

 21  Chromosomes contain ChromosomeSegments. This representation differents 
 22  from the canonical composite structure in that we don't really have 
 23  'leaf' nodes here -- all components can potentially hold sub-components. 
 24
